Parameter‑efficient fine‑tuning (PEFT) methods often rely on fixed module architectures. This work introduces arithmetic composition of PEFT modules, allowing dynamic combination and reuse of learned adaptations. The approach improves efficiency and flexibility while maintaining strong performance across diverse tasks.
